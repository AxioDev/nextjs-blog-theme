## Confidentiality Policy

Welcome to the confidentiality policy of **Smart Text Transformer**, a Chrome extension leveraging AI technology for text reformulation.

### Data Collection

**Smart Text Transformer** does not collect any personal data. The text you choose to reformulate is processed on-the-fly and is not stored on any servers.

### Data Usage

The text provided for reformulation is sent to our secure server only for the purpose of processing your request. It is not used for any other purposes and is not shared with any third parties.

### Security

We implement advanced security measures to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data. All communications between the extension and our server are encrypted.

### Changes to Our Confidentiality Policy

We reserve the right to modify this confidentiality policy at any time. Any changes will be promptly communicated on this page.
